Leadership and Vision

Under Mayor Michael Rama’s leadership, Cebu City has undergone rapid transformation. With a focus on sustainability, inclusive growth, and community engagement, Mayor Rama has implemented numerous projects to improve the quality of life for the people of Cebu City. His initiatives are aimed at boosting the city’s infrastructure, public services, and economic development.

Key Achievements

  • Development of modern transport systems
  • Establishment of new parks and green spaces
  • Improvements to the city's healthcare facilities

Future Vision for Cebu City

  1. Transforming Cebu into a global tourism hub
  2. Promoting environmental sustainability
  3. Creating more job opportunities for local residents
