Infrastructure and Development

Under Governor Gwendolyn Garcia's leadership, Cebu has seen rapid development in terms of infrastructure. Key road projects and transportation systems have greatly improved connectivity across the province, making it easier for residents and tourists alike to travel.

Key Infrastructure Projects

  • Expansion of major highways and roads
  • Development of new bridges
  • Improvement of ports and airports for better access

Healthcare & Education Initiatives

  1. Construction of new healthcare centers in rural areas
  2. Development of more public schools to accommodate students
  3. Improvement of public hospitals with state-of-the-art equipment
