Key Entrepreneurial Support Initiatives

Vice Mayor Garcia understands the vital role of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the economy and has implemented various programs to help them grow.

  • Financial Assistance Programs

    • Providing micro-financing options, low-interest loans, and grants to entrepreneurs, particularly those in the food, technology, and agriculture sectors.


  • Business Incubators:

    • Establishing spaces for start-ups to collaborate, innovate, and access mentoring from experienced business professionals.


  • Skills Development Training

    • Organizing workshops on entrepreneurship, financial management, and marketing to equip local entrepreneurs with the necessary tools for success.


  • Market Access and Networking

    • local and international trade fairs, business expos, and online platforms where entrepreneurs can showcase their products and connect with potential buyers and partners.


  • Support for Women Entrepreneurs

    • Special initiatives aimed at empowering women in business, including workshops, networking events, and access to financial resources.

Future Plans

The Vice Mayor plans to:

  1. Introduce a "Start-up City" program, where young innovators can pitch their ideas for government funding and support.
  2. Launch a "Cebu City Business Accelerator" to guide entrepreneurs in scaling their businesses to new markets.
  3. Establish mentorship hubs in key districts to assist entrepreneurs with practical advice and solutions to common business challenges.
