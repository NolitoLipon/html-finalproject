Key Smart City Projects

Through these smart city initiatives, Vice Mayor Raymond Alvin N. Garcia has improved public services, infrastructure, and connectivity for residents, ensuring Cebu Cityâ€™s competitiveness on the global stage.

  • Smart Traffic Management

    • A new traffic system using real-time data to monitor and manage traffic flow, significantly reducing congestion during peak hours.


  • Free Public Wi-Fi

    • The installation of Wi-Fi hotspots in public spaces such as parks, transport terminals, and government buildings to promote digital inclusion.


  • Digital City Portal

    • A centralized digital platform for citizens to access government services, pay taxes, and report issues directly to local authorities.


  • Smart Waste Management

    • Introduction of sensors in waste bins to optimize garbage collection routes, reducing traffic, improving efficiency, and keeping the city cleaner.


  • Smart Street Lighting

    • LED streetlights with motion sensors that adjust brightness based on pedestrian and vehicle traffic, saving energy and improving safety.


Impact on Residents

These initiatives not only improve the quality of life for residents but also reduce environmental impact by integrating green technologies. Citizens benefit from better services, enhanced safety, and more efficient infrastructure.

Future Goals

Vice Mayor Garcia is committed to further expanding the Smart City projects with plans for:

  1. Increasing the number of smart schools and e-learning facilities in partnership with local universities.
  2. Developing smart health systems for more efficient medical services, including telemedicine for remote communities.
  3. Promoting sustainable development with eco-friendly technologies in construction and urban planning.
