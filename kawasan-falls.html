About Kawasan Falls

Kawasan Falls is a stunning three-tiered waterfall located in Badian, Cebu, Philippines, and is renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters. Nestled amidst lush tropical greenery, it offers a serene and picturesque escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. The falls cascade into natural pools, creating perfect spots for swimming and bamboo rafting. One of its unique features is the vibrant blue color of the water, attributed to the limestone deposits in the area. Visitors can enjoy activities like canyoneering, which involves trekking, jumping, and climbing through the rugged terrain leading to the falls. The sound of the rushing water, combined with the tranquil surroundings, provides a calming and rejuvenating experience. How to Get There

Take a bus or car from Cebu City to Badian. From there, a short trek leads you to the falls.
