Overview of Cebu City Government

The Cebu City Government is committed to enhancing the lives of its people through effective governance. Under the leadership of Mayor Michael Rama, Cebu City has made significant strides in urban development, infrastructure, and public services. The city is known for its bustling economy, rich culture, and commitment to sustainable growth.

Key Programs and Initiatives

  • Affordable housing projects for the urban poor
  • Improved traffic management systems
  • Support for local entrepreneurs and businesses

Recent Developments

  1. Expansion of the city's road networks
  2. Enhancements to healthcare facilities
  3. Increased investment in tourism and events
