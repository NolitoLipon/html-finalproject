Expanding Public Services

Ensuring that all Cebu residents have access to essential services is a priority. The government has made significant strides in improving healthcare, education, utilities, and other essential services that cater to the needs of the population.

Public Service Enhancements

  • Establishment of more government service offices in rural areas

    • The establishment of more government service offices in rural areas ensures that residents have better access to essential public services.

    By bringing government sevices closer to rural communities, this initiative helps reduce travel time and promotes more inclusive development.


  • Improvement of the social welfare system to support marginalized communities

    • The improvement of the social welfare system helps provide essential resources and opportunities for those in need.

    strengthening social safety nets, the government can reduce inequality and empower disadvantaged groups to improve their quality of life.


  • Expansion of waste management and environmental sanitation programs

    • The expansion of waste management and environmental sanitation programs ensures cleaner and healthier communities by reducing pollution and waste buildup.

    These programs promote proper waste disposal, recycling, and sanitation practices that protect both public health and the environment.
