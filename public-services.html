Expanding Public Services

Ensuring that all Cebu residents have access to essential services is a priority. The government has made significant strides in improving healthcare, education, utilities, and other essential services that cater to the needs of the population.

Public Service Enhancements

  • Establishment of more government service offices in rural areas
  • Improvement of the social welfare system to support marginalized communities
  • Expansion of waste management and environmental sanitation programs

Future Plans for Public Services

  1. Improvement of public safety through more police and fire stations
  2. Enhancement of water and electricity services to underserved areas
  3. Increase in government support for small businesses and startups
