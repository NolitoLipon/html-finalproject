About Oslob Whale Sharks

Oslob, a coastal town in Cebu, Philippines, is famous for its unique whale shark watching experience. Known locally as "butanding," these gentle giants attract tourists from all over the world who come to witness their grace and majesty up close. Visitors can snorkel or dive alongside the whale sharks in their natural habitat, creating a once-in-a-lifetime encounter. The clear waters of Oslob make it an ideal spot for observing these magnificent creatures, whose sheer size and gentle demeanor leave spectators in awe. Strict guidelines are in place to ensure the safety and conservation of the whale sharks, promoting sustainable tourism practices. Beyond the whale shark experience, Oslob offers additional attractions like Tumalog Falls and heritage sites, making it a must-visit destination in Cebu. The harmonious coexistence between humans and marine life in Oslob is a testament to the importance of respecting and preserving the environment.

Activities

  • Snorkeling and swimming with whale sharks

  • Photography sessions


  • Exploring nearby Tumalog Falls

How to Get There

Oslob is a three-hour drive from Cebu City. Tour packages are also available for convenience.
