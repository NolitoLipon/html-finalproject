Preserving Cebu's Environment

Cebu's government is dedicated to implementing various environmental programs aimed at improving sustainability. With a focus on clean energy, waste management, and biodiversity conservation, the province is committed to protecting its natural resources for future generations.

Key Environmental Projects

  • Establishment of recycling and waste management programs
  • Development of protected marine reserves
  • Promotion of renewable energy projects

Future Environmental Goals

  1. Increasing the use of solar energy in government buildings
  2. Protecting and restoring forests and mangroves
  3. Advocating for eco-friendly tourism and sustainable agriculture
