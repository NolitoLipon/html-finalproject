About Malapascua Island

Malapascua Island, located off the northern tip of Cebu, Philippines, is a popular destination known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. The island is famous for being one of the few places in the world where divers can regularly encounter the endangered thresher sharks, particularly at Monad Shoal, making it a top spot for diving enthusiasts. Malapascua offers a laid-back atmosphere, with its quiet, palm-fringed beaches and small, friendly local community. Aside from diving, visitors can enjoy snorkeling, island hopping, and exploring the island’s picturesque coastline. The island is also home to beautiful coral gardens, perfect for underwater exploration. Malapascua is a haven for those seeking tranquility, adventure, and an unforgettable experience with nature, while still embracing the warmth and hospitality of the Filipino people. Its blend of natural beauty and unique marine life makes Malapascua Island a must-visit destination in Cebu.

Activities

  • Scuba diving with thresher sharks

  • Snorkeling in coral gardens

  • Sunset watching
