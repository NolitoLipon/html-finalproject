Governor Gwendolyn Garcia's Leadership

Governor Gwendolyn Garcia has been instrumental in making Cebu a model of governance. Her commitment to the welfare of the people, as well as her initiatives on infrastructure and public services, has significantly improved the province. She has also made efforts to enhance Cebu’s reputation on the global stage.

Governor Garcia’s Achievements

  • Development of key infrastructure projects
  • Promotion of agricultural initiatives to boost local farmers
  • Enhancement of education and healthcare services in rural areas

Her Vision for Cebu

  1. Focus on sustainable tourism
  2. Expansion of economic development programs
  3. Boosting the province’s resilience to natural disasters
