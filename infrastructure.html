Major Infrastructure Developments

Cebu’s infrastructure has undergone a major transformation in recent years. The provincial government and local authorities have prioritized improving roads, public transportation, and utility systems to accommodate the growing population and boost economic activity.

Key Infrastructure Projects

  • The construction of the Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway (CCLEX)

    • The construction of the Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway (CCLEX) significantly improves transportation connectivity between Cebu City and the island of Mactan.

    This infrastructure project is expected to ease traffic congestion and boost economic growth in the region by facilitating smoother travel for commuters and businesses.


  • Rehabilitation and expansion of Mactan Cebu International Airport

    • The rehabilitation and expansion of Mactan Cebu International Airport aim to accommodate increasing passenger traffic and enhance the travel experience.

    This project is expected to improve connectivity, support tourism growth, and boost the local economy by providing modern and efficient airport facilities.


  • Upgrade of the Cebu Port to enhance trade and tourism

    • The upgrade of the Cebu Port aims to enhance trade and tourism by improving the capacity and efficiency of cargo and passenger services.

    This development will strengthen Cebu’s position as a major hub for both international commerce and leisure travel in the region.


Future Plans

  1. Further expansion of the Cebu North Bus Terminal

    2. The further expansion of the Cebu North Bus Terminal aims to accommodate the growing number of commuters and improve transportation efficiency.

    This development will provide better facilities and services, reducing congestion and enhancing the overall travel experience for passengers.


  2. Developing new industrial parks for business and manufacturing

    3. Developing new industrial parks for business and manufacturing will attract investment and stimulate economic growth in the region.

    These industrial parks provide the necessary infrastructure and facilities for businesses to operate efficiently and create job opportunities for local communities.


  3. Continuing investment in renewable energy sources for Cebu

    4. Continuing investment in renewable energy sources for Cebu will help reduce the region's dependence on fossil fuels and promote sustainability.
