Expanding Educational Opportunities

Cebu has made great strides in enhancing its educational infrastructure, providing better access to quality education, and ensuring that students have the tools and resources they need to succeed in life.

Key Educational Projects

  • Construction of new public schools in rural areas
  • Establishment of scholarship programs for high school and college students
  • Upgrading facilities in state universities for a modern learning environment

Future Goals

  1. Establishing more technical-vocational schools
  2. Increasing access to online learning programs
  3. Promoting partnerships with international educational institutions
